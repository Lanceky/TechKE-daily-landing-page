PRIVACY POLICY — TechKE Daily

Last updated: June 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how TechKE Daily ("TechKE Daily", the "App", "we",
"us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the TechKE
Daily mobile application and related services (the "Service"). By using the
Service, you agree to the practices described in this policy.


1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

a) Information you provide when you sign in.
The App lets you browse without an account. If you choose to sign in, you may
authenticate using a third-party provider such as Google or GitHub. Through that
provider we receive basic profile information, which may include your name,
email address, and profile picture. We do not receive or store your social
account password.

b) Content and activity data.
To personalize your experience, we store information such as the channels you
follow, articles you bookmark, and your in-app preferences (for example, theme
and notification settings). Some of this data may be associated with your
account or with an anonymous device identifier if you are not signed in.

c) Push notification tokens.
If you enable notifications, we collect and store a push notification token so we
can deliver notifications (such as the daily top stories) to your device. You
can disable notifications at any time.

d) Device and usage information.
We may automatically collect limited technical information such as device type,
operating system version, app version, general app interaction events, and a
non-permanent device identifier. This helps us operate, secure, and improve the
Service.

e) Advertising data.
In the ad-supported (free) version, our advertising partners may collect
information such as advertising identifiers, device information, and ad
interaction data to serve and measure advertisements.

f) Purchase information.
If you buy the "Remove Ads" product, the purchase is processed by Google Play.
We receive confirmation that the purchase was made so we can unlock the ad-free
experience, but we do not collect or store your payment card or billing details.


2. HOW WE USE INFORMATION
We use the information we collect to:
  - Provide, maintain, and personalize the Service and your feed.
  - Authenticate you and manage your account.
  - Deliver push notifications you have opted into.
  - Display and measure advertisements (free version) and process the "Remove
    Ads" purchase.
  - Operate, secure, debug, and improve the Service.
  - Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.


3. HOW WE SHARE INFORMATION
We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as follows:
  - Service providers and infrastructure. We use Supabase for authentication,
    database, and backend services, and Expo / platform push services to deliver
    notifications.
  - Identity providers. Google and GitHub process your sign-in when you choose
    to authenticate with them.
  - Advertising partners. Appodeal and its mediated ad networks may process data
    to serve and measure ads in the free version.
  - App stores. Google Play processes purchases.
  - Legal reasons. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect
    the rights, safety, and security of our users or the Service.


4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES AND LINKS
The Service links to articles and content hosted on third-party websites. When
you open an external article, the third party's own privacy policy applies. We
encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services and
websites you interact with. Relevant third-party policies include:
  - Google: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  - GitHub: https://docs.github.com/site-policy
  - Supabase: https://supabase.com/privacy
  - Appodeal: https://www.appodeal.com/privacy-policy/
  - Expo: https://expo.dev/privacy


5. DATA RETENTION
We retain account-related information for as long as your account is active or as
needed to provide the Service. Bookmarks, follows, and preferences are retained
until you remove them or delete your account. Push tokens are retained while
notifications are enabled and are removed or refreshed when you disable
notifications or uninstall the App.


6. YOUR CHOICES AND RIGHTS
  - Notifications. You can enable or disable push notifications from the App's
    settings or your device settings at any time.
  - Account data. You can remove bookmarks and unfollow channels within the App.
  - Account deletion. You may request deletion of your account and associated
    personal data by contacting us at the email below. We will process such
    requests in accordance with applicable law.
  - Advertising. You can limit ad tracking through your device settings, or
    remove ads entirely by purchasing the "Remove Ads" product.
Depending on your location, you may have additional rights such as access,
correction, or deletion of your personal data. Contact us to exercise these
rights.


7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
The Service is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age
of digital consent in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal
information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal
information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.


8. DATA SECURITY
We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your
information, including encrypted connections and access controls through our
infrastructure providers. However, no method of transmission or storage is
completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.


9. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS
Your information may be processed and stored on servers located outside your
country of residence, including by our service providers. Where required, we
take steps to ensure your information receives an adequate level of protection.


10. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material
changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate,
provide notice within the App. Your continued use of the Service after changes
take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
